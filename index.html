The Graduation Songs

The subunit graduation songs were released on June 22, 2018, as bonus CDs rewarding the purchase of all of the Love Live! Sunshine!! Season 2 Blu-rays. The three songs are sung by the three subunits of Aqours. Common themes include reflecting on the past, enjoying the present, and looking torwards the future.
